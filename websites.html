OUR STORY

KANKONG CHIPS ORIGINAL

Welcome to Kangkong Chips Original, the home of healthy and delicious chips made from locally sourced kangkong, or water spinach. Our story began with a simple desire to provide Filipinos with a healthier snacking alternative that is both delicious and affordable.
